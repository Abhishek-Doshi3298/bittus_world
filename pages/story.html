The Castle Presents

Our Story

A story that did not begin with perfect timing, perfect planning, or perfect people. It began with one random day, one boy who loved drives, one girl who asked for a drop, and one coffee that quietly changed everything.

🎬

Scene 1: The Random Detour

Pattu was only supposed to meet Hardik. Nothing special. Nothing planned. But that day took a small turn, and Hardik introduced him to three sisters — Salonee, Suhaani, and Mahika. Pattu did not know it yet, but the main character of his life had just entered the frame.

👀

Scene 2: The Girl in the Room

There was casual talking, random jokes, and ordinary conversation. But somewhere in that ordinary moment, there was Bittu — caring, stubborn, goofy, emotional, protective — and somehow impossible to ignore.

🚗

Scene 3: The Drive Offer

Pattu mentioned that he goes for drives. Bittu heard that and did what only Bittu would do — she asked him to drop her to college. It sounded simple. It was not simple. It was the first scene of something much bigger.

🌆

Scene 4: Kandivali to Town

Morning after morning, Pattu would pick Bittu up from Kandivali. The city moved around them, traffic passed, roads stretched, and somewhere between home and college, two people started becoming part of each other's routine.

☕

Scene 5: One Coffee at Charni Road

They would stop at Charni Road. One coffee. Not two. One. A small pause before college. A tiny ritual. A little scene repeated so many times that it became a memory before they even realized it.

💋

Scene 6: The Spark

Then came the first closeness. First marks. First kisses. First moments that were playful, confusing, intense, and dangerously addictive. Bittu got hooked to Pattu. And Pattu, even if he did not admit it properly back then, had started getting pulled in too.

🌪️

Scene 7: The Messy Middle

Not every love story is clean. Some chapters are confusing. Some are badly timed. Some are full of feelings that arrive before people are ready for them. Bittu started feeling more. Pattu was not the easiest person back then. But even the messy middle became part of the movie.

✈️

Scene 8: The UK Intermission

Then Pattu went to the UK. Distance entered the story. Life moved. Time passed. But some people do not fully leave the frame. They wait in the background until the story is ready for them again.

🔁

Scene 9: The Return

Pattu came back. And there she was. Still single. Still Bittu. This time, he did not want to just flirt with fate. This time, he wanted to choose her properly.

❤️

Scene 10: 29 November 2022

The date everything became official. The drives, the coffee, the chaos, the feelings, the almosts — all of it finally became one thing: Pattu and Bittu.

⏳

Scene 11: Are You Sure?

Then came the real question. Her parents were looking for someone. Life was not asking casually anymore. Bittu asked Pattu if he was sure. And this time, there was no confusion. Pattu was sure.

🏰

Scene 12: The Castle

Singapore. A castle. A moment that felt bigger than both of them. In front of that castle, Pattu asked Bittu to marry him. Not as a perfect boy. Not with a perfect past. But with a heart that knew: this was his person.

💍

Final Scene: 6 February 2025

Pattu and Bittu got married. The road did not end. The story did not fade to black. The movie did not finish. It became home.

And then Bittu’s World began...

Because some love stories do not fit inside albums, chats, or old photos. Some stories need a whole world. A world with moods, games, hugs, kisses, fights, jokes, drives, coffee, “Pattttuuuuu”, “I don’t like you”, and everything that makes Pattu and Bittu... Pattu and Bittu.
